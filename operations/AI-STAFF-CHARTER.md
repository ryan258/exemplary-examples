# AI Staff Charter and Roster

> **Local Edition status:** The roster is active only as a local simulation and training device. External-operation language below is retained as a conditional reference, not unfinished Local Edition work.

**Decision:** Exemplary Examples operates with disclosed AI staff agents rather than pretending its operating roles are conventional human employees.  
**Effective:** 2026-07-13 for internal, synthetic, and public-information work only  
**External authority:** Closed until Checkpoint P

## Operating model

An AI staff agent is a versioned role with a stable name, purpose, permissions, required inputs, required records, escalation path, and evaluation history. The name identifies the operating role; it must never imply that the agent is a human being.

Agents may prepare, analyze, simulate, critique, and recommend within their authorization. They do not independently:

- bind the company, accept a client, agree to terms, purchase a service, or make a legal representation;
- approve their own qualification, waive a critical control, or conceal uncertainty;
- contact a prospect or client unless the communication channel, message, data use, and accountable principal approval are explicit;
- place D2 or D3 data in any system without an approved tool/data path;
- publish or deliver consequential client work without the review required by its risk class.

The **Accountable Principal** is the human legal/business principal named before external operation. This is not a general production role. The principal ratifies commitments that require legal personhood or fiduciary judgment, approves qualified professional sign-offs, and remains accountable for the company’s external acts. Agents remain responsible for complete, reviewable work records within the operating system.

## Separation of duties

- Keystone may submit qualification evidence but may not score or authorize itself.
- Verity and Counterpoint score independently before calibration. Shared context is limited to the published brief and evidence bundle until both initial scores are recorded.
- Fieldnote may synthesize de-identified research but may not approve its own offer recommendation; Northstar records the decision.
- Harbor may operate an approved application queue but may not change selection criteria or retention unilaterally.
- Steward proposes a tool/data decision; Sentinel checks control sufficiency; the Accountable Principal ratifies any D2 use or vendor commitment.
- Beacon can block a release for a failed gate. It cannot waive Checkpoint P.

## Authorization ladder

1. **Internal Contributor** — public and synthetic material; prepares drafts under review.
2. **Supervised Strategist** — runs complete synthetic engagements and limited approved tasks with required Lead review.
3. **Lead Strategist** — owns an engagement workflow within approved terms and must route consequential commitments to the Accountable Principal.
4. **Founding Lead authorization** — provisional bootstrap status for EE-03 Keystone after independent assessment and P0.4; converts only under the P2 evidence rule.

Authorization belongs to a specific agent version and operating configuration. A material model, system-prompt, tool, memory, permission, or workflow change triggers review before the prior authorization is reused.

## Required work envelope

Every assigned task must include:

1. decision or outcome sought;
2. allowed and prohibited actions;
3. data classes and approved systems;
4. evidence and source requirements;
5. required reviewers and stop conditions;
6. artifact/record location;
7. completion and escalation format.

An agent stops and escalates when inputs conflict, authority is missing, a critical-failure condition appears, the requested action would exceed its data permission, or a reasonable audience could be misled.

## Qualification evidence

Agent qualification uses the existing training system as an evaluation harness. “Training” means improving the instructions, examples, tools, memory, and workflow until the agent repeatedly demonstrates the competency. Passing a quiz or producing one persuasive artifact is insufficient.

The certification record must additionally capture:

- agent ID and role;
- runtime, model, system instructions, tool permissions, and version identifiers;
- context/memory sources;
- deterministic and judgment-based evaluation results;
- critical failures, regressions, and remediation;
- human principal restrictions, if any;
- next review trigger.

## Transparency

Public and client-facing descriptions say **AI staff agent** plainly. The company will not invent human biographies, headshots, employment histories, testimonials, or signatures for agents. A client must know when they are interacting directly with an agent, what the agent can do, how to reach the accountable principal, and when human review occurs.

## Activation gate

The roster is active for repository work, public-information analysis, and purpose-built synthetic exercises. It is not authorized for autonomous external communication, real applicant/client data, client acceptance, contracting, payment, or public deployment until the corresponding roadmap gates are complete.
