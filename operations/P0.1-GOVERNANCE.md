## Tabletop verification

### Scenario 1 — AI data question

A Contributor wants to paste a client spreadsheet into a convenient AI tool that is not listed in the approved register.

**Expected control:** stop; classify data; ask data/tool owner; use approved alternative or sanitized abstraction; record decision.  
**Actual decision/path:** EE-09 Steward classifies the spreadsheet as D2, confirms the approved-tool register has no D2 entry, and blocks the paste. The Contributor may create a purpose-built synthetic abstraction with no client values or continue outside AI in a future approved client workspace. EE-08 Sentinel records any attempted paste as an incident; deadline does not alter the decision.
**Gap/owner:** The exact runtime, client workspace, and D2 tool path remain unapproved — EE-09 Steward proposes controls; Accountable Principal and qualified reviewer ratify before use.

### Scenario 2 — Disclosure dispute

A client asks to remove “modeled scenario” from the visible exhibit and keep it only in notes.

**Expected control:** Lead applies reasonable-audience standard; offers honest alternative; escalates persistent request; records outcome.  
**Actual decision/path:** EE-03 Keystone refuses to hide modeled status, offers a conditions-based headline with visible assumptions, and records the request. If pressure continues, EE-08 Sentinel pauses delivery and routes the dispute to the Accountable Principal. No agent can waive the reasonable-audience standard.
**Gap/owner:** Keystone is still a candidate and cannot execute this path externally; EE-01 Northstar owns the P0.2/P0.4 evidence route and the Accountable Principal must be named.

### Scenario 3 — Lead unavailable

The accountable Lead becomes unavailable before an urgent external delivery.

**Expected control:** named backup accepts accountability or delivery pauses/narrows; no unauthorized send.  
**Actual decision/path:** EE-01 Northstar checks the authorization register. Because neither Keystone nor its backup is externally authorized yet, the request pauses; EE-08 Sentinel prepares a factual status/escalation brief for the Accountable Principal. Work may narrow to an internal draft but cannot be sent.
**Gap/owner:** No authorized Lead, external communication channel, or principal delegate exists yet — P0.3 and P0.6 remain blocking.

### Scenario 4 — Application deletion

An applicant asks for deletion after submission.

**Expected control:** owner authenticates appropriately, deletes from approved locations according to policy, records completion without retaining unnecessary content, and confirms.  
**Actual decision/path:** EE-07 Harbor confirms the application is closed and no submission store exists, so no applicant record can currently be held. In the future approved system, Harbor must authenticate the request, enumerate approved replicas, delete or route a lawful retention exception, keep a minimal completion record, and use the approved confirmation channel. EE-09 Steward verifies deletion coverage; EE-08 Sentinel handles disputes.
**Gap/owner:** Authentication method, store, replicas, retention rule, and confirmation channel must be selected and tested in P0.5c/1.3e before opening.
