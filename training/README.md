# Exemplary Examples™ Strategist Certification

This directory contains the internal training system for consultants and strategists who will deliver real Exemplary Examples™ engagements.

The program is competency-paced. Learners advance by producing evidence, not by completing a fixed number of hours.

## Start here

1. Read the [training system specification](01_foundations/TRAINING-SYSTEM-SPEC.md).
2. Review the [curriculum and pathway](01_foundations/CURRICULUM.md).
3. Use the [consultant handbook](01_foundations/CONSULTANT-HANDBOOK.md) as the operating reference.
4. Complete the instruction and activities in [course modules](02_instruction/COURSE-MODULES.md).
5. Use the [workshop deck outlines](03_live-practice/WORKSHOP-DECKS.md) and [simulation packs](03_live-practice/SIMULATION-PACKS.md) for live practice.
6. Use the [assessment and certification guide](04_assessment/ASSESSMENT-AND-CERTIFICATION.md) to prepare evidence.
7. Begin client exposure only through the [supervised-practice guide](05_supervised/SUPERVISED-PRACTICE.md).
8. Facilitators should also use the [facilitator guide](06_facilitation/FACILITATOR-GUIDE.md).
9. For the first synthetic qualification run, use the [P0.2 run plan](dry-runs/P0.2-RUN-PLAN.md) and the [EE-MVQ-01 controlled fixture](dry-runs/fixtures/EE-MVQ-01/README.md).

## Certification levels

| Level | May do | May not do |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Contributor | Research, draft, classify evidence, and run internal QA under review | Own client commitments or send unreviewed external work |
| Supervised Strategist | Conduct supervised intake and delivery work within an approved scope | Independently own an Executive Retainer or waive review gates |
| Lead Strategist | Own retainers, approve delivery, supervise learners, and participate in assessment | Bypass evidence, data, disclosure, or certification policy |

Certification defines authorization, not seniority or personal worth. A learner may be advanced in one competency and still need supervision in another.

## Core operating rule

> If an audience could reasonably mistake constructed material for a verified real-world fact, the strategist must change the labeling, sourcing, or artifact before delivery.

Satirical products may be fully fictional. Professional consulting work must clearly distinguish sourced facts, client-provided facts, calculations, inferences, and modeled scenarios.

## Accessibility

Competency is the standard; a specific physical or communication method is not. Facilitators must offer equivalent ways to participate in live, timed, spoken, handwritten, or visually dense activities. Examples include additional processing time, advance materials, written participation, captioning, accessible source files, breaks, assistive technology, and an alternate demonstration format that measures the same competency.

Accommodation use is not recorded as a performance defect and does not lower the rubric standard.

## Curriculum versioning

Use a simple release identifier:

```text
EE-TRAINING-YYYY.MM
```

Every certification record includes the curriculum version. Material changes to certification rights, critical failures, or assessment thresholds require a new version and a review of active certifications.
