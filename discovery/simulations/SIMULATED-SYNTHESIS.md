## Repeated patterns (synthetic)

### Problem and current behavior

1. The real pain is in *sourcing a defensible comparable* and *marking sourced-vs-modeled*, not in exhibit craft.
2. It concentrates in solo consultants (own the argument + the spend) and internal strategy (recurring + budget).
3. "Adjacent" roles (presentation, procurement) either sit downstream of the problem or don't own it at all.

### Trust and disclosure

1. The truth standard is welcomed by everyone who owns the argument — but for internal buyers it's the
   *entry ticket*, not the selling point.
2. Burned buyers (P3) trust a *named human*, not a documented system alone.
3. The disclosed-AI-staff framing is a credibility problem exactly for the sensitive work where need is highest (P5).

### Brand language

**Language that creates trust:** "accountable principal," "documented review systems," "explicit professional
truth standard," "sourced examples, disclosed composites."
**Language that loses trust:** "We build yours," "Every business case needs one perfect example,"
"openly satirical brand," "disclosed AI staff agents" (for sensitive buyers).
**Frequent misunderstanding:** The tagline is read as *fabrication-as-a-service* on first contact (P2, P3, P6).
The satire and the remedy are being read as the same thing — a repeated framing failure the reveal ordering
does not fully fix.

### Offer and buying process

**Preferred engagement shape:** Retainer for recurring-need buyers (P1, P5); on-demand/overflow for agencies (P2).
**Credible turnaround expectation:** 48–72h (solo consultant) to 1–2 weeks (sensitive internal precedent work).
**Buying owner and approval needs:** Solo consultant self-approves; internal lead has a budget line but needs
legal/finance above a threshold; agency director only recommends.
**Competing budget/alternative:** Subcontractor designers + analyst subscriptions (P1); a boutique retainer +
internal headcount (P5).

## Simulated proceed/narrow/revise/stop leaning

**Leaning (SIMULATED): Narrow + Revise.**

- **Narrow the customer** to the two segments that showed real need and a buying path: independent strategy
  consultants and internal strategy leads. Agencies are conditional-and-white-label-gated; presentation
  consultants are suppliers not buyers; procurement-adjacent is no-fit.
- **Revise the framing.** The satire repeatedly read as fabrication near clients and procurement (P2, P3, P6),
  and the "disclosed AI staff agents" line eroded credibility for the highest-value sensitive work (P5).
  Lead with the accountable *human* principal and the truth standard; make the satire recede (or disappear)
  in client- and governance-facing contexts.
- **Do NOT proceed** to a pilot on this basis. Even setting aside that it's synthetic, only 1 of 6 gave a
  concrete follow-up — below the real ≥2 bar.
