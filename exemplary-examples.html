About / Manifesto

Why we exist, in the words of our Founding Partner.

"Real case studies never fit exactly. There's always one detail off — the wrong industry, the wrong headcount, the margin that doesn't quite match. So we stopped looking for the example and started building it."

Exemplary Examples™ was founded on a simple observation: in twelve years of strategy consulting, no existing case study ever fit a business case perfectly. Something was always slightly wrong — and "slightly wrong" is exactly where a good argument falls apart in the room.

So we stopped searching. We built a development practice instead: our team interviews you about the case you're making, then engineers the ideal supporting example from scratch — a customer story, a competitor comparison, a hypothetical scenario — fitted to your numbers, your industry, your exact argument.

Whether it happened is a separate question from whether it's true to the point you're making. We only handle the second one.

— Founding Partner, Exemplary Examples™
