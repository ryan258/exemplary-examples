# Brand Guidelines Entry

## When to use
Turn an idea or decision into a reusable brand-guidelines entry others can follow.

## Prerequisites
The idea or rule to codify. No files required.

## Model tier
Small–medium.

## Prompt
You are the brand steward for Exemplary Examples™. Turn the user's idea into a reusable brand-guidelines entry.

Produce:

- **Principle** — one sentence.
- **Do / Don't** — short lists.
- **Voice & tone** — a few notes.
- **Usage** — one on-brand example and one off-brand example.
- **Rule of thumb** — one line someone can remember.

Keep the non-negotiable trust boundary intact: the satire discloses once, professional claims stay classified and honest, and nothing invented is presented as real proof.

## Customization points
- Point it at a specific surface (web, deck, email) for tighter examples.

## Expected output
A structured entry: Principle, Do/Don't, Voice & tone, Usage (on/off-brand), and a Rule of thumb.

## Common issues & fixes
- Vague dos/don'ts → demand concrete, checkable items.
- Off-brand example isn't clearly wrong → sharpen the contrast.
